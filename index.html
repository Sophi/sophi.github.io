Why This Matters

Your team is too close to the product. You've been living with its quirks for months. You know the workarounds. You've rationalized the compromises.

But your customers won't. They'll notice immediately. They'll return it. They'll write reviews. They'll tweet about it.

An external validation catches what internal testing misses:

  • Real-world usage patterns you didn't anticipate
  • Edge cases that seem "unlikely" but happen constantly
  • Compatibility issues across different environments
  • User experience problems that seem obvious in hindsight
  • The small details that separate good products from great ones

Better to catch it before launch than explain it after.
